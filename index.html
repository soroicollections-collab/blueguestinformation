Welcome

Welcome to Soroi Blue

Whether you are just beginning your journey along the coast, or gently bringing it to a close, we are truly delighted to have you with us. Here, on the pristine shores of Diani Beach, we hope you will find a place to pause, to breathe, and to simply feel at home.

With just eleven rooms, Soroi Blue is intentionally intimate — crafted to feel less like a hotel, and more like your own private retreat by the sea. A place where you are known, where your preferences matter, and where every detail is quietly taken care of.

Our team is here for you in the most natural and unobtrusive way — whether it's arranging a morning by the ocean, a quiet corner for sunset, or simply ensuring your favourite drink is always close at hand. Whatever you need, whenever you need it, please allow us the pleasure of looking after you.

We invite you to settle in, unwind, and let the rhythm of the coast carry you.

Beyond the Property

Discover & Drift

Diani and Mombasa offer a beautiful mix of coast, culture, and gentle adventure — perfect for drifting a little further while you're with us. Our team arranges everything. Ask the hospitality desk for the current excursions menu and availability.

01

At the Coast

Signature Experience

Diving & Snorkelling

Kenya's coral reef along the south coast is one of the most varied in East Africa. Dive the fringing reef at Kisite-Mpunguti, or simply snorkel off the beach. The water is warm, clear, and full of life year-round.

Ask reception for current diving operators and snorkelling conditions — the reef is best at low tide.

Deep Sea Fishing

Tuna, marlin, and sailfish. Half-day and full-day charters available from Diani's fishing boats.

Whale Sharks & Dolphins

Seasonal encounters in open water. Ask the team for current conditions before you book.

Bush Baby Feeding

Every evening at 7pm — these wide-eyed nocturnal creatures emerge from the coastal forest. A magical moment, especially for little ones.

Skydiving over Diani

Tandem jumps with panoramic Indian Ocean views. Diani is one of the best skydiving locations in East Africa.

Golf

18-hole championship course, 5 minutes from the property. Hire available on site.

Yoga & Wellness

Complimentary yoga mats available on request for beach or garden practice. Ask the hospitality desk to arrange your session.

Sea Turtle Conservation

15 minutes away. Discover local efforts to protect endangered sea turtles nesting along Kenya's coastline — a meaningful and memorable visit.

Ocean Tribe Diving

10 minutes away. Professional scuba diving and snorkelling excursions on the coral reefs of the Indian Ocean. All levels welcome.

Ngalawa & Glass Bottom Boat

Departs from the beachfront. Explore the reef and coastline aboard a traditional local dhow or glass-bottom boat — no diving experience needed.

02

Nature & Culture

Colobus Conservation

The Diani forest is home to one of Kenya's most rare primates — the black and white colobus. The Colobus Conservation centre offers guided walks and close encounters with the forest and its resident wildlife.

Kaya Kinondo

An ancient sacred forest on the coast, centuries old and still spiritually alive. Guided walks with local Digo community members reveal stories that the trees have kept for generations.

Kongo Mosque

One of the oldest mosques on the Kenyan coast — a working place of worship and a window into the deep Swahili history of this coastline. Modest dress required. Not open to women.

Community Initiatives

Conservation and community projects operate along this stretch of coast. Ask the manager directly if you'd like to get involved or make a contribution during your stay.

03

Day Trips from Diani

Wasini Island & Kisite-Mpunguti

A full day by dhow — snorkelling over the coral garden, dolphins in open water, and a long, unhurried Swahili seafood lunch on the island. One of the best days the south coast offers.

Shimba Hills National Park

Forest elephants, rare roan antelope, and the Sheldrick Falls hike — all within two hours of Diani. A completely different landscape from the coast.

Fort Jesus & Mombasa Old Town

A UNESCO World Heritage Site — the old Portuguese fort overlooks the harbour where dhows have traded for centuries. Walk into Mombasa Old Town for the carved doors, spice markets, and the layered history of the Swahili coast.

Funzi Island

Peaceful canoe trips through mangrove channels and a quiet island lunch — the kind of half-day that stays with you. A world away from the open ocean, and only an hour's drive south.

Local Markets & Shopping

Diani Beach Shopping Centre, Carrefour, Muthaiga Mini Market, and Chandarana Foodplus are all within 10–15 minutes. Vibrant craft markets are nearby too — ask the team for a map and honest advice on what's worth your time.

Diani After Dark

Diani's evenings have their own character — beach bars, live music, and nightlife venues worth exploring. Ask reception for current recommendations and a safe way to get there and back.

Our team arranges all activities and day trips. Ask the hospitality desk for the current excursions menu, availability, and pricing. Advance notice of 24 hours is appreciated for most outings.

Fire Safety

In the event of a fire, act swiftly and calmly. Fire exit maps are displayed in each room.

01

Call — Dial +254 (0) 738 537 093 immediately to report the fire to our team.

02

Evacuate — Quickly and safely make your way to the nearest fire assembly point. Fire exit maps are displayed in each room.

03

Stay Alert — Once at the assembly point, remain calm and await instructions from the Soroi Blue team. Do not re-enter the building.

04

First Aid — Trained staff and a first aid kit are available on site. Kenya emergency services: 999.
