Signature Experience Diving &
Snorkelling

Kenya's south coast reef is one of the most varied in East Africa — warm, clear, and alive year-round. The fringing reef stretches the full length of Diani Beach, making snorkelling directly off the sand a remarkable experience even for first-timers.

For a deeper encounter, Kisite-Mpunguti Marine National Park lies 45 minutes south by boat — one of the most biodiverse marine environments in the Indian Ocean. PADI-certified dive operators work out of Diani daily. Beginner courses and guided reef dives are available subject to conditions. The reef is healthiest and most colourful between October and March, though rewarding throughout the year.

Ask reception for current dive operators, snorkelling conditions, and the best tides to go.

Sport Fishing Deep Sea
Fishing

The Indian Ocean off Diani is among Kenya's most productive deep-sea fishing grounds. Tuna, marlin, sailfish, and wahoo are regularly landed from these waters — with peak season for large billfish running from October through March.

Half-day and full-day charters depart from the beach with experienced captains and all tackle provided. Catch-and-release is encouraged and available on all trips. Whether you are a seasoned angler or stepping onto a charter for the first time, an early morning on the open ocean here is a pursuit worth making time for.

Half-day charters typically depart 06:30 and return by midday. Full-day departures available on request.

Arrangements Half-day and full-day charters. All tackle and equipment provided. Arranged through our concierge — rates on request.

Open Water Encounters Whale Sharks & Dolphins

Whale sharks visit Kenya's south coast seasonally — gentle giants in open water, an encounter few people ever forget. Spinner and bottlenose dolphins are resident year-round and frequently encountered on offshore trips. Conditions and sightings vary — the team will advise on current activity before you book.

Arrangements Seasonal — ask our concierge for current conditions. Rates on request.
Reef Discovery Glass Bottom Boat

A traditional dhow fitted with a glass hull panel — a gentle, captivating way to explore the Diani reef without getting wet. Coral gardens, sea turtles, and shoals of reef fish are visible directly beneath the boat. Perfect for children, non-swimmers, or anyone who simply wants to watch. Sunset dhow sailings also available.

Extraordinary Adventure Skydiving over the Ocean

Diani is one of a very small number of places in Africa where you can freefall over a beach — the Indian Ocean stretching to the horizon, the reef clearly visible from altitude, the coastline curving away in both directions below you.

Tandem jumps operate from Ukunda Airstrip, just minutes from Soroi Blue. No experience is required. A brief ground training session is followed by the flight to altitude and a freefall descent over the coast. The landing is on the sand.

Whether it is on your list or not, this is one of those experiences that stays with you. Widely regarded as one of East Africa's most spectacular skydiving locations.

Arrangements Tandem jumps — no prior experience required. Departs from Ukunda Airstrip. Arranged through our concierge — rates on request.

Signature Day Trip Wasini Island & Kisite

The most celebrated full-day excursion from Diani. A traditional dhow departs in the early morning heading south to Kisite-Mpunguti Marine National Park — one of the finest snorkelling sites on the African coast. Dolphins are frequently encountered in open water on the crossing.

After a morning on the reef, the dhow sails to Wasini Island — a tiny, car-free Swahili settlement where lunch is served on a shaded terrace above the sea. Freshly caught seafood, crab, coconut rice, and the unhurried flavours of the coast. The return journey brings you back as the afternoon light turns gold.

Full day. Departs early morning, returns mid-afternoon. Lunch included.

Arrangements Full-day excursion including lunch and snorkelling equipment. Arranged through our concierge — rates on request.

National Reserve Shimba Hills

Forty minutes inland lies a lush forested plateau — a completely different landscape from the coast. Shimba Hills is home to forest elephants, the rare roan antelope, sable, and an extraordinary range of coastal forest birds. The Sheldrick Falls hike is the highlight: a guided walk through indigenous forest to a beautiful waterfall deep in the reserve.

Arrangements Half-day or full-day. Private vehicle and guide. Arranged through our concierge — rates on request.
UNESCO World Heritage Site Fort Jesus & Old Town

Built by the Portuguese in 1593 at the edge of Mombasa's Old Town, Fort Jesus is one of the most significant monuments on the East African coast. The Old Town surrounding it is a living network of carved Swahili doorways, spice traders, and layered history best explored on foot with a local guide. One of Kenya's most rewarding cultural visits.

Arrangements Full day. Private transfer from Soroi Blue. Guided tour available. Arranged through our concierge — rates on request.

Marine Conservation Sea Turtle Conservation

Fifteen minutes south along the coast, this working turtle hatchery supports nesting sea turtles along the Diani shoreline. Visits include a guided tour of the hatchery and — during nesting season — the possibility of seeing eggs or hatchlings up close. A meaningful and memorable visit for guests of any age, directly connected to the health of the reef.

Arrangements 15 minutes from Soroi Blue. Our concierge can arrange transport — rates on request.
Forest Wildlife Colobus Conservation

The Diani coastal forest is one of the last habitats of the black-and-white colobus monkey — one of Kenya's rarest primates. Colobus Conservation runs guided forest walks and offers close encounters with resident troops. The centre also rehabilitates other coastal wildlife. A short, rewarding visit that connects guests directly to local conservation work happening on this stretch of coast.

Arrangements Short drive from Soroi Blue. Walkable for the adventurous. Arranged through our concierge — rates on request.
